Small Businessess, Individuals and Families: Welcome!

 Singles

Taxpayers file form 1040; itemizers use Schedule A. Standard deduction for taxpayers who do not itemize is $12,950.

 Head of Household

Unmarried taxpayers with dependents file as Head of Household (HOH); itemizers use Schedule A. Standard deduction for taxpayers who do not itemize is $19,400.

 Families

Couples could file as Married Filing Jointly (MFJ), or Separately (MFS). Standard deduction for MFJ who do not itemize is $25,900 or, $12,950 for MFS.

 Rental Properties

Special discount coupons available.

 Active Military/Clergy

Special coupons discount available.

 Sole Proprietors

Taxpayers file form 1040 with schedule C.

 Partnerships

They are pass-through entities who file Form 1065. The entity issues Schedule K-1 to the general partner and, or limited partners.

 S-Corporations

They are pass-through entities who file form 1120-S. The entity issues Schedule K-1 to all shareholders.

Taxes to Go

It is a "secured" link to a mobile app in the cloud used to download the EVCI custom-branded app either from the Google Play Store, or Apple App Store, onto the taxpayer phones. EVCI's brand name appears at the top of the app. Click the link below if your would like to sign up for our waiting list, or start your tax return.

Taxes to Go

By clicking the link above, taxpayers can use the app to do the tasks listed below which are made from the comfort of the taxpayer's house, or anyother location that has access to the Internet without ever setting foot in our office.

  • Start their own returns
  • Upload documents (W2’s, Drivers Licenses, and other tax docs)
  • Send messages
  • Receive the completed return
  • Sign the return electronically

To download your customized app, please enter your Name, Email, Referral Code (if you have one), phone number and we'll send you the app via text message straight to your phone as shown on the adjacent figure.

After we receive the form, we will send you a personal link/URL (via text or email) which you simply click it to download your mobile app.

Customer Portal

The customer portal is another "secured" place in the cloud that allows taxpayers to sign their tax return documents without ever visiting our office. EVCI will prepare your tax return, create the customer portal and send the documents to you to sign from anywhere. Please note that taxpayers "must choose" either the Customer Portal or Taxes to Go.
